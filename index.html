C-Arches: Addressing Reproductive Coercion in Health Settings

What do you know about violence prevention?

Everyone deserves to make their own reproductive choices. This is called Reproductive Autonomy. C-ARCHES is an app that is designed to help you understand the connections between your health and relationships, how to take control of your health and wellbeing, and how to get help for yourself and/or loved ones, when you’re ready.

Community Resources and Support

Everyone deserves to be in a healthy and respectful relationship(s). Sometimes that doesn't always happen. If you are being hurt by your partner, it is NOT your fault.

Hover over the boxes below to see some resources that can help you learn more about your options and what is best for YOU.

The National Domestic Violence Hotline
The National Domestic Violence Hotline has staff who are trained to help people in unsafe relationships. They are available 24/7, and can help you plan for safety.
  • Phone: 1-800-799-SAFE (1-800-799-7233)
  • Website: www.thehotline.org
Crisis Text Line
Text trained counselors about anything that's on your mind.
  • Phone: Text “START” to 741741
  • Website: www.crisistextline.org
myPlan app
If you, or someone you care about is experiencing abuse in their relationship, download the myPlan app to help with safety decisions.
  • Website: www.myplanapp.org
Futures Without Violence
Futures Without Violence is a health and social justice nonprofit organization that provides support, education, and resources for survivors of violence.
  • Website: www.futureswithoutviolence.org/
